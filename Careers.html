    Construction Engineer/Inspector

    by Jeanne Farah | Dec 8, 2015 | Job Opportunities, News |




    Farah and Sons, Inc. is a small construction inspection firm in Indianapolis and surrounding areas. We are immediately hiring a Construction Engineer/Inspector for a roadway/bridge inspection project about one hour in the Indianapolis area.


    The successful candidate will:


  • Be responsible for providing daily inspection of roadway and bridge construction work to ensure that the project construction complies with plans and specifications.
  • Complete daily reports of inspection work.
  • Monitor work of contractors-quality assurance.
  • Keep superiors advised of problem areas.
  • See that work conforms to plans and specifications.
  • Perform concrete testing.

    • Requirements:


  • Must be able to stand for long periods of time.
  • Must be able to perform standard mathematical calculations, measurements, etc.
  • Must be willing to travel throughout Indiana.

    • Although this position requires knowledge of construction principals, new Construction Management and Civil Engineering graduates are encouraged to apply. Those with INDOT certifications are encouraged to apply.



    Position qualifies for full benefit package including health insurance.
