Quality Work by a Family-owned Company.


Farah & Sons, Inc. is a small, locally-owned, family-run company which specializes in construction inspection.


Ken Farah, President of Farah and Sons, Inc. is a civil engineer by education and trade. His years of experience began in Boston, MA as an engineer on the Big Dig, the largest, most complex, and technologically challenging urban infrastructure project in American history, estimated to cost nearly $14 billion at completion. Since moving to Indianapolis in 2002, Ken has worked for both Indiana Department of Transportation and Malcolm Pirnie, Inc.

Farah & Sons, Inc. provides quality engineering services and construction management. We are committed to hiring dedicated, experienced employees who have a strong work ethic. As a result, we put our time and resources into the services we provide, ensuring our clients that the work we do will be reliable and focused. We strive for excellence in everything we do.
